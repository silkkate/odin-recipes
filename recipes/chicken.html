Instant Pot Sticky Korean Chicken

Description

This delicious Korean-inspired chicken makes the perfect weeknight dinner. It comes together quickly in the Instant Pot and is very mucch a set it and forget it type of recipe.

Gochujang, a Korean red pepper paste, is the star of the show, giving the chicken its signature spicy-sweet flavor and making for an excellent sticky sauce.

Source:

halfbakedharvest.com

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup low sodium soy sauce or tamari
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1-2 tbsp. gochujang
  • 2 tbsp. toasted sesame oil
  • 1 inch fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, grated
  • 1 1/2 lbs. boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • steamed white rice, for serving
  • 1 cup kimchi, for serving
  • 1 sliced mango, for serving
  • toasted sesame seeds and green onions, for serving

Steps

  1. In the bowl of your instant pot the soy sauce, honey, Gochujang, sesame oil, ginger, and garlic.
  2. Add the chicken, tossing to coat. Cover and cook on high pressure/manual for 8 minutes. Once done cooking, use the quick release valve and release the steam.
  3. If needed to thicken the sauce, set the instant post to sauté and cook 10 minutes or until the sauce thickens and coats the chicken. If the sauce seems thin, just continue to cook on sauté, checking often until the sauce is your desired consistency.
  4. To serve, spoon the chicken and sauce over bowls of rice. Toss the kimchi with the mangos and serve alongside the chicken. Sprinkle toasted sesame seeds and green onions. Enjoy!
