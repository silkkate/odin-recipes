The Best NYC Gingersnap Cookies

Description

These are my personal favorite cookies in all of New York City. You can find them at the holiday markets atBryant Park or Union Square, and outside of the holiday season, you can also purchase them at the farmers' market at Union Square on Wednesdays and Saturdays year-round.

These cookies are large, super soft and chewy, and have the perfect balance of ginger spice and sweet and smoky molasses. The sugar topping adds a delicious slight crunch. These cookies are sure to convert any ginger hater!

Source: delish.com

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp. ground ginger
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/3 cup molasses (Grandma's is a good brand)
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup granulated sugar, plus more for rolling
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp. pure vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, ginger, cinnamon, salt, baking powder, baking soda, and cloves.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together oil, molasses, sugars, egg, and vanilla. Add dry ingredients to wet and mix until just combined. Refrigerate dough for 1 hour.
